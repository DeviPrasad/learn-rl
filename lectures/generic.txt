These are not identically distributed because they have different means (0.2, -0.8, 1.6, 0.4).
For i.i.d., all must come from the same distribution.
In this case, they don't.

Pulling Arm 1 doesn't affect Arm 2's distribution
- Getting a reward from Arm 3 doesn't change Arm 4's mean
- The arms are causally independent
- No interaction or influence between arms

The rewards associated with the K bandit arms is a collection independent and identically distributed (IID) variables.
